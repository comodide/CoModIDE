Summary

Pattern 1d is a transformational process that takes an InputRole of either artifact-type data or artifact-type symbol which will then participate in a process-type transform to produce an OutputRole of artifact-type data.

Explanations

  1. Disjointness: Artifact is not InputRole.

  2. Range: The range of performsInputRole is InputRole.

  3. Inverse Existential: For every InputRole, there has to be an inverse R-Filler

  4. Qualified Scoped Functionality: For every Artifact, there is at most one R-filler in InputRole

  5. Inverse Qualified Scoped Functionality: for every InputRole, there is at most one inverse R-filler in Artifact

  6. Disjointness: Artifact is not OutputRole.

  7. Range: The range of performsOutputRole is OutputRole.

  8. Inverse Existential: For every OutputRole, there has to be an inverse R-Filler

  9. Qualified Scoped Functionality: For every Artifact, there is at most one R-filler in OutputRole

  10. Inverse Qualified Scoped Functionality: for every OutputRole, there is at most one inverse R-filler in Artifact

  11. Disjoint: Process is not InputRole

  12. Domain: Any value in Process can be assigned to any variable with accepts

  13. Scoped Domain: The domain of accepts, scoped by InputRole, is Process

  14. Range: the range of accepts is Process

  15. Existential: Every Instance of Process must be related through the relation accepts to some instance within class InputRole

  16. Qualified Scoped Domain: For every Process there is at most one R-filler in InputRole

  17. Inverse Qualified Scoped Functionality: For every InputRole there is at most one inverse R-filler in Process

  18. Disjoint: Process is not OutputRole

  19. Domain: Any value in Process can be assigned to any variable with produces

  20. Scoped Domain: The domain of produces, scoped by OutputRole, is Process

  21. Range: the range of produces is Process

  22. Existential: Every Instance of Process must be related through the relation produces to some instance within class OutputRole

  23. Qualified Scoped Domain: For every Process there is at most one R-filler in OutputRole

  24. Inverse Qualified Scoped Functionality: For every OutputRole there is at most one inverse R-filler in Process
